If your remote isn't working or isn't pairing properly

  1. Check your batteries and replace them as needed.
  2. Press and hold the Home button on the remote for 3 seconds.
  3. Navigate to Xumo settings and attempt to Sync remote.
  4. Unpair the remote by pressing and holding the Back and Add (+)  buttons together for three seconds until the LED is persistent green.
  5. Press 984 on the remote.
  6. Navigate to Xumo settings and attempt to Sync remote once more.
